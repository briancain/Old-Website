    Bio


    I am a Masters student in Computer Science at Kansas State University. My focus will be related to computer and software security, although I have not picked a thesis yet. I am also a part of the INSIGHT GK-12 Program at Kansas State for Summer 2012 - Spring 2013.

    I am currently a member of the Kansas State Cyber Security Club. I was the first team captain for a competition team that traveled to Denver, Colorado and participated in the Rocky Mountain Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition.

    I was a member of the Kansas State Marching Band for 3 years, a member of the Kansas State Jazz Combos for 1 year, and part of the Kansas State Concert Jazz Ensemble for 4 years including this semester. I play the tenor saxophone and acoustic guitar. I love drinking loose leaf tea in my adagio teas brewer, and I am slowly becoming a coffee connoisseur as well. When I'm not in school, I enjoy Power Lifting at a local gym.
